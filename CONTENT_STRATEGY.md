# Credit Vivo v14 Full Content Strategy

## Slogan
You take control. We clear the path.

## Public promise
Credit Vivo helps customers understand, build, clean up, track, and learn credit one step at a time.

## Customer-facing words
Use:
- Credit Check-In
- Credit Roadmap
- Monthly Action Plan
- Score Opportunities
- Clean-Up Progress
- Learning Center
- Updates
- Extra Support

Avoid public-facing:
- Parser
- Metro 2
- FCRA engine
- Violation codes
- Dispute all
- Legal escalation as a primary message
