# WEBFLOW AI / DESIGNER BRIEF — CREDIT VIVO

## Project
Redesign Credit Vivo public website visually using existing content.

## Brand
Credit Vivo
Slogan: You take control. We clear the path.

## Main Problem to Fix
The current site content is good, but the pages feel too text-heavy and not visually balanced. We need images, illustrations, dashboard cards, softer spacing, and calmer font ratios.

## Design References
Look at the general balance of service websites like:
- Sky Blue Credit
- Dovly
- Credit Saint
- CreditRepair.com

Use them only as inspiration for pacing, spacing, and trust-building. Do not copy exact designs, text, images, sections, icons, layouts, colors, or competitor branding.
