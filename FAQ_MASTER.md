# Credit Vivo FAQ Master

## What is Credit Vivo?
Credit Vivo is a guided credit improvement platform that helps you understand, build, clean up, track, and learn your credit step by step.

## What does “You take control. We clear the path.” mean?
It means you stay in charge of your credit journey. Credit Vivo helps organize the steps, explain what matters, and guide you through the process.

## Does Credit Vivo guarantee score increases?
No. Credit Vivo does not guarantee score increases, removals, approvals, or specific outcomes.

## What is a Credit Check-In?
It is your free starting point. Credit Vivo reviews your credit profile and gives you a simple roadmap.

## Why not dispute everything at once?
Disputing everything at once can be confusing, expensive, and not always the smartest first step. Credit Vivo helps prioritize actions.

## Online disputes or letters?
Online can be useful for simple issues. Detailed issues may need custom letters, evidence, and tracking.

## What is collection validation?
Collection validation means asking selected collectors to confirm important details about a debt, such as the original creditor, balance, ownership, and authority to collect.

## What does verified mean?
It means the bureau or reporting company said the item should remain. Credit Vivo helps review whether follow-up makes sense.

## Can accurate negative information be removed?
Accurate, current, and verifiable information may remain on a credit report. Credit Vivo focuses on organization, education, possible errors, and next steps.

## How does Credit Vivo help me build credit?
Credit Vivo helps you understand score-building habits like paying on time, lowering card balances, avoiding unnecessary applications, and tracking due dates.

## Is Credit Vivo only for disputes?
No. Credit Vivo is built around credit guidance, education, score opportunities, clean-up support, and tracking.

## Is Credit Vivo a law firm?
No. Credit Vivo is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice. Optional legal support may be available separately.

## Can I cancel?
Yes. Credit Vivo should provide clear membership terms and a clear cancellation process.
