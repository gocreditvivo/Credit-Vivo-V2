GLBA Privacy Notice

Last updated: June 21, 2026

What We Collect

We may collect nonpublic personal information such as identity details, credit-report information, account information, uploaded documents, payment information, and communications.

How We Share

We may share information with service providers that help us provide requested services, process payments, send communications, store documents, prepare disputes, or comply with law.

How We Protect Information

Credit Vivo should maintain a written information security program, restrict access, use secure vendors, and monitor systems that store sensitive customer information.

Opt-Out

If Credit Vivo shares information in a way that gives customers an opt-out right under applicable law, customers may submit a privacy request or email support@creditvivo.com.
