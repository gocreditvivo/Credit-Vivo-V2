FAQ

Clear answers before signup.

Does Credit Vivo guarantee removals?

No. Credit repair results are not guaranteed. Credit Vivo focuses on reviewing inaccurate, unverifiable, outdated, or questionable items and keeping the process transparent.

Is Credit Vivo a law firm?

No. Attorney escalation may be available for eligible cases through partner consumer-rights attorneys. Attorney involvement depends on review, eligibility, and the law firm's process.

What happens after the free scan?

You receive a recommended path: Core, Plus, or Attorney Tier. A specialist can then help confirm the right next step.
