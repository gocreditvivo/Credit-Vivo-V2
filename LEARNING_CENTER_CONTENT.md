# Credit Vivo Learning Center Master

## What affects your score?
Category: Beginner

Payment history, balances, credit age, new applications, and account mix all matter.

## Why payment history matters
Category: Beginner

Late payments can hurt because lenders want to know if you pay on time.

## Why balances matter
Category: Beginner

High credit card balances can hurt your score profile even if you pay every month.

## When should I pay my card?
Category: Beginner

Paying before the statement date may help a lower balance report.

## What is a credit report error?
Category: Clean-Up

An error may be information that is wrong, incomplete, outdated, duplicated, or not yours.

## What is a collection?
Category: Clean-Up

A collection means a debt may have been sent or sold to a collector. It should be reviewed carefully.

## What is collection validation?
Category: Clean-Up

It means asking selected collectors to confirm important debt details before bigger actions.

## Why not dispute everything at once?
Category: Clean-Up

A step-by-step plan can avoid confusion, unnecessary cost, and weak actions.

## What does verified mean?
Category: Progress

Verified means the item stayed after review. It does not always mean follow-up is impossible.

## Why track everything?
Category: Progress

Tracking helps you know what was sent, what is pending, and what to do next.

## Before buying a car
Category: Goals

Lower balances, avoid unnecessary applications, and review your report before applying.

## Before buying a home
Category: Goals

Start early, keep payments on time, lower balances, and avoid new debt.
