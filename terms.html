Last updated: June 21, 2026

This MVP page is a compliance draft. Credit Vivo should have counsel review and approve final wording before accepting payments or pulling credit reports.

Services

Credit Vivo provides credit report organization, credit education, AI-assisted issue spotting, document management, and dispute preparation support. Services may include customer-authorized disputes to credit bureaus or furnishers.

No Guarantee

Credit Vivo does not guarantee a score increase, credit approval, deletion of accurate information, loan approval, credit limit increase, or any specific result.

Customer Responsibilities

  • Provide accurate identifying information and documents.
  • Review draft disputes before submission when approval is required.
  • Tell Credit Vivo if a report item is accurate, settled, paid, fraudulent, or already disputed.

Credit Reports and Authorization

By using services that require credit report access, the customer authorizes Credit Vivo and its service providers to access, process, and analyze credit-report information for the requested service.

Cancellation

If Credit Vivo services are subject to the Credit Repair Organizations Act, customers have the right to cancel the contract without penalty within three business days after signing.
