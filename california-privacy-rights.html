Your California Privacy Rights

Last updated: June 21, 2026

California residents may have rights to know, access, correct, delete, opt out of sale or sharing, limit certain sensitive personal information uses, and not be discriminated against for exercising privacy rights.

How To Submit a Request

Use the Privacy Requests page or email support@creditvivo.com. Credit Vivo may need to verify identity before responding.

Sensitive Information

Credit Vivo may process sensitive information such as credit reports, Social Security information, IDs, addresses, and financial account details only as needed to provide requested services, maintain security, or comply with law.

Authorized Agents

California residents may use an authorized agent where permitted by law. Credit Vivo may require proof of authorization and identity verification.
