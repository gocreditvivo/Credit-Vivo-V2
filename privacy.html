Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Information We Collect

We may collect contact information, identity documents, credit reports, account details, uploaded files, payment information, dispute history, chatbot messages, communications, and website usage data.

How We Use Information

  • Provide credit analysis and dispute support.
  • Verify identity and protect accounts from fraud.
  • Prepare, send, and track customer-authorized disputes.
  • Communicate service updates, reminders, and support messages.
  • Comply with legal, regulatory, tax, security, and recordkeeping duties.

Sharing

We may share information with service providers, payment processors, credit-report access providers, mailing providers, legal or compliance support, bureaus, furnishers, and regulators when needed to provide services or comply with law.

Portal, Email, And SMS Updates

Credit Vivo may use your contact information to send service updates, document reminders, dispute-status alerts, response notices, billing notices, support messages, and review requests where permitted and where you have provided required consent.

The portal should contain the full update. Email and SMS alerts should use limited, generic language and direct you to sign in, so sensitive credit-report details are not exposed in an inbox or text message.

Chat Assistant

Credit Vivo may offer a chat assistant to help customers navigate the portal, understand general next steps, and find education or support resources. Chat messages may be logged for security, quality, support, and compliance purposes. Customers should not enter Social Security numbers, full account numbers, or other unnecessary sensitive details into chat.

Security

Credit Vivo should use access controls, encryption, audit logging, and limited internal access for credit reports, IDs, and sensitive customer documents.

Your Choices

Customers may request access, correction, deletion, opt-out, or privacy support by using the Privacy Requests page or emailing support@creditvivo.com.
