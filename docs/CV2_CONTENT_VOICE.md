# Credit Vivo Content Voice

## Default Public Voice

Keep it simple. Say the result. Use everyday customer words.

Credit Vivo public copy should sound direct, bold, and easy for normal customers to understand.

## Homepage Language

Use customer words like:

- Fix what's hurting your score.
- Repair credit report errors.
- Remove inaccurate negative accounts.
- Challenge bad tradelines.
- Build your AI Credit Boost Plan.
- Track progress.
- Get ready for better loans and financing.

## Results First

Every page section should answer:

- What result does the customer want?
- How does Credit Vivo help them get there?
- What should they do next?

Customer results to emphasize:

- Higher score goals
- Negative accounts fixed or challenged
- Credit report errors repaired
- Bad tradelines challenged
- Better car loans
- Better mortgage readiness
- Better apartment approval readiness
- Better financing options
- Attorney support when needed

## Do Not Weaken Homepage Copy

Do not lead the homepage with legal-style wording.

Avoid vague front-page phrases like:

- possible reporting issue
- work toward
- credit position
- opportunity readiness
- consumer reporting problem

These can be used in disclosures, legal pages, or internal documents only.

## Compliance Placement

Compliance language belongs in:

- disclosures/
- terms
- privacy
- footer
- checkout
- signup consent
- customer approval screens
- admin review workflow

The homepage should sell the result clearly.

## Attorney Content

Attorney support stays strong, but should be positioned as a power layer.

Use:

- Attorney support when needed.
- Attorney support for unresolved credit-reporting issues.
- We help organize your file for attorney review.

## Final Rule

Bold public message. Strong customer result. Simple words. Compliance behind the scenes.
